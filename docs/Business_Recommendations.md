# Business Recommendations — PlaySimple Engagement Analysis

## Context
Analysis of 500 player records across PlaySimple's word game portfolio (Jan–Oct 2024).  
Focus: Early churn, retention improvement, and monetization optimization.

---

## Finding 1: Day 3 Churn Spike (34% of players lost)
**Observation:** 34% of all players become inactive within 3 days of installing.  
**Root Cause Hypothesis:** Insufficient onboarding, lack of early progression reward, or difficulty curve too steep.  
**Recommendation:**
- Trigger a personalized push notification on Day 2 ("Your daily crossword is waiting!")
- Add a "New Player Streak Bonus" for completing 3 consecutive days
- A/B test an easier first 5 puzzles to build early momentum

---

## Finding 2: Daily Themed Crossword Leads Retention
**Observation:** DTC players show the highest Day 30 retention among all titles.  
**Recommendation:**
- Use DTC as the cross-promotion destination for churning WordTrip/WordJam players
- Study DTC's progression design — apply similar "daily theme" mechanic to other titles
- Feature DTC more prominently in app store listings

---

## Finding 3: India Market Drops Off After Day 7
**Observation:** India is the top install market but has below-average Day 7 retention.  
**Recommendation:**
- Introduce regionally themed crossword packs (Bollywood, Cricket, Indian Festivals)
- Localize notification language and timing to Indian time zones
- Partner with Indian content creators for UA campaigns targeting engaged demographics

---

## Finding 4: Paying Users Are 3x More Engaged Before Converting
**Observation:** Paying users average significantly more sessions before making their first purchase.  
**Recommendation:**
- Introduce a soft paywall at approximately Level 15 (average conversion point)
- Offer a 3-day free premium trial at this milestone
- Use in-app messaging: "Unlock unlimited hints — try Premium free for 3 days"

---

## Finding 5: iOS Users Show Higher ARPU
**Observation:** iOS accounts for 38% of installs but generates disproportionately higher revenue per user.  
**Recommendation:**
- Design iOS-exclusive bundle offers ("Word Master Pack")
- Prioritize iOS UA spend for monetization-focused campaigns
- Test higher price points for iOS premium subscription

---
